I am an astrophysicist focused on stellar evolution, modeling, and astronomy outreach. My work combines quantitative research with public communication and visual storytelling.

Research

My research centers on stellar evolution and inference methods, including MCMC modeling and interpolation of stellar evolutionary tracks.

I am particularly interested in subgiant branch stars, helium enrichment, and connecting theory to observations.
